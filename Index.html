Who is Varun Mayya?

Varun Mayya is an entrepreneur, venture capitalist, neuroeconomist, computer scientist, blockchain Pioneer aspiring oncologist and CRISPR enthusiast. A lot of things have been achieved by him at a very young age.

Varun Mayya's entrepreneural journey started when he was pursuing his bachelors degree from Manipal Institute of Technlogy. When he was in his first year of college, Varun started with a t-shirts start-up. Then he began freelancing and when he was in his final year at MIT he founded a recruitment platform named SIZR.

He is the youngest venture capitalist. He is currently the CEO at Avalon Labs which is a holdings company.

He is trying to bring a change in the education system by empowering everyone with quality education for free at his new venture known as meta. Although it is still in beta stage. Though I could not get access to it but there has been a lot of buzz in his telegram group. As Varun said that the meta platform is designed to be entertaining and not boring like the traditional education system.

What is meta?

Varun is trying to gamify education by creating teams, and levels. Students can choose their mentors. For business they have to opt for Varun Mayya, coding - Jasneet Singh Sawhney, marketing - Abhinav Arora and Finance - Sashank Udupa. Varun once mentioned in an interview that he will be using horse blinders so that the users can stick to one category. After they reach a certaon level they will be allowed to access other categories. In this way it will help the users as they will not be jumping from one skill to the other. This will enable the users to learn a particular skill properly before they can start learning a new skill.
